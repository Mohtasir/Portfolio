To activate the contact form, you need a free EmailJS account. Follow these steps:

  4. In this file, replace the 3 placeholder values in the <script> section at the bottom: YOUR_PUBLIC_KEY, YOUR_SERVICE_ID, YOUR_TEMPLATE_ID
